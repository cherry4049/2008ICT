Frequently Asked Questions:

Your bill may be higher due to seasonal changes, a malfunctioning appliance, or increased usage. Check your Usage page to pinpoint which appliance is using the most energy.

Go to Settings, click on "Appliances", and check the boxes for appliances you have in each room.

Go to the Alerts page and toggle on "Abnormal usage warning". You'll receive notifications when your usage spikes.

Go to Settings, scroll to the bottom, and click "Delete My Account". You'll be asked to confirm before deletion.
